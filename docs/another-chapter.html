There are two main suspected factors of obesity:

  • Poor exercise and/or
  • Poor diet

However studies find that ‘you cant outrun a bad diet’ meaning the main contributor of this condition is controlled by the food market. This combined with the fact that obesity is a modern disease must mean that the cause was introduced fairly recently. Here are some factors we will examine in relation to Obesity to investigate further.

  • Diet (ratio of what foods)
  • GDP
  • Historical change
  • Lifestyle
  • Daily Calories available/consumed
